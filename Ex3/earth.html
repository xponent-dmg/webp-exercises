Planet Earth

Overview

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It has a diameter of about 12,742 km and is the densest planet in the Solar System.

Key Facts

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Distance from Sun: ~149.6 million km
  • Atmosphere: 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace gases
  • Natural Satellite: The Moon
